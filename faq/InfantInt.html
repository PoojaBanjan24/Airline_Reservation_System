• Domestic Flights:-
    • Hand Baggage- One hand bag up to 7 kgs and 115 cms (L+W+H), shall be allowed per customer. For contactless travel we recommend to place it under the seat in front, on board
    • Check-in Baggage- 15kg per person (1 piece only, not exceeding more than 32Kgs per baggage) effective Oct 1st, 2020. For Double or MultiSeats bookings, extra 10 kg. Additional charges may apply for excess baggage.
  • Infant Baggage Allowance:-
  • Domestic Travel:-
    • Hand Baggage- One hand bag up to 7 kgs and 115 cms (L+W+H), shall be allowed per customer. For contactless travel we recommend to place it under the seat in front, on board.
    • Check In Baggage -NIL
  • International Travel:-
    • Hand Baggage- One hand bag up to 7 kgs and 115 cms (L+W+H), shall be allowed per customer. For contactless travel we recommend to place it under the seat in front, on board.
    • Check In Baggage-NIL

    One stroller or baby pram per infant is allowed without any charge

  • International Flights:-
    • Ishuja Domestic Sector connecting to Ishuja international sector or vice versa: Ishuja's Free Baggage Allowance for International sector will be applicable
    • Ishuja Domestic Sector connecting to another airline to/from an international destination: 15 Kg per person (One piece only, not exceeding more than 32Kgs per baggage). Additional charges will apply for excess baggage.
